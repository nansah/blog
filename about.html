The Full Story

From a small Instagram account to a full-time creative life

I moved to New York City chasing a feeling more than a plan — and somewhere between the subway commutes, the rooftop sunsets, and the never-ending search for the perfect little black dress, I started documenting it all. What began as a place to save my own outfit photos slowly turned into a community of people who felt the same pull toward the city that I did.

These days, I split my time between writing, styling, filming, and — most importantly — actually living the life I write about. I believe in trying the reservation-only restaurant, taking the scenic subway route, and never buying anything that doesn't feel like "me." This space is where I share all of it: the wins, the outfit repeats, the hard lessons, and the hidden gems worth telling you about.

Thank you for being here. Whether you found me through a single Reel or you've been reading since the very first post, I'm so glad you're part of this.
