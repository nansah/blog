The Full Story

Stepping away from expectations to walk in purpose

Hi, I'm Nana Yaa! As a first-generation Ghanaian American, I chose to step away from unspoken expectations to pursue my true calling. Today, I'm passionate about helping millennial women do the same — to live boldly and intentionally, and walk confidently in their God-given purpose.

With a background as a celebrity stylist assistant and experience in PR and marketing, I've had the opportunity to work both behind the scenes and in front of the camera. I now combine that industry experience with my love for storytelling to create content that inspires women to elevate their everyday through fashion, faith, and intentional living.

Whether I'm sharing outfit inspiration, home styling tips, or mindset shifts, my goal is to encourage women to pursue the abundant life they were created for and becoming their best selves along the way.
