Disclosure

Transparency about affiliate links, sponsored content, and gifted products.

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, this page discloses my relationships with brands and how I may earn income from this Site.

2. Affiliate Links

Some links on this Site — including in the Shop My Faves section and within blog posts — are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, I may earn a small commission, at no extra cost to you. I only recommend products I genuinely use and love; commission never influences whether something makes it into a recommendation.

3. Sponsored Content & Gifted Products

From time to time, I partner with brands on sponsored posts, or receive gifted products in exchange for consideration. When a post is sponsored or includes gifted items, it will be clearly marked as such within the post itself. My opinions remain my own regardless of whether content is sponsored or gifted — I only work with brands I'd genuinely recommend to a friend.

4. Advertising

This Site displays advertising through Google AdSense. Ads are served by Google and its partners and may be personalized based on your browsing activity — see the Cookie Policy for details on how to control this.

5. How to Identify Sponsored Content

Sponsored posts, gifted-product mentions, and affiliate-linked content are labeled within the post itself (for example, with a note at the top of the article or near the relevant product). If you're ever unsure whether a piece of content involves a paid partnership, feel free to ask.

6. Questions

If you have questions about a specific post, partnership, or link, email hi@nanayaaansah.com or use the contact form.
