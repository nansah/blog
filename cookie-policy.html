Cookie Policy

What cookies this site uses, and how to control them.

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use it.

2. How I Use Cookies

This Site uses cookies and similar technologies (like local storage) to keep the site running smoothly, measure traffic, and — where applicable — show relevant advertising.

3. Types of Cookies Used

  • Essential cookies — required for core site functionality, such as remembering your session in the admin dashboard.
  • Analytics cookies — set by Google Analytics to understand how visitors navigate the Site (pages viewed, time on site, etc.).
  • Advertising cookies — set by Google AdSense to serve and personalize ads, and measure ad performance.
  • Functional cookies — set by Cloudflare Turnstile to verify that form submissions come from real people rather than bots.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services embedded on the Site (Google Analytics, Google AdSense, Cloudflare Turnstile). These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, which I encourage you to review:

  • Google's Cookie Policy
  • Cloudflare's Cookie Policy

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings — most browsers let you block cookies entirely or clear existing ones. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect how parts of the Site function.

To opt out of personalized advertising specifically, visit Google Ad Settings or the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page. To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Me

Questions about cookies on this Site? Email hi@nanayaaansah.com or use the contact form.
