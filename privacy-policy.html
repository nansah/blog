Privacy Policy

How I collect, use, and protect your information on this site.

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nana Yaa Ansah ("I," "me," or "my") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit nanayaaansah.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information I Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Your name and email address when you sign up for the newsletter, download a free resource, or submit the contact form.
  • Any message content you send through the contact form (e.g. company name, inquiry type, and your message).

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data such as pages visited, time spent on the Site, referring URLs, browser type, and device information, collected via Google Analytics.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies — see the Cookie Policy for details.

3. How I Use Your Information

  • To send the newsletter, requested downloads (like the Wardrobe Audit Worksheet), and respond to inquiries.
  • To understand how visitors use the Site and improve its content and performance.
  • To serve relevant advertising through Google AdSense.
  • To detect and prevent spam or abuse (e.g. via Cloudflare Turnstile verification on forms).

4. Third-Party Services

The Site relies on the following third-party services, each of which may process your data under its own privacy policy:

  • Google Analytics — website usage analytics.
  • Google AdSense — displays advertising and may use cookies to personalize ads.
  • Resend — sends newsletter and transactional emails, and stores subscriber lists.
  • Supabase — hosts the Site's database (blog content, form submissions).
  • Cloudflare Turnstile — verifies that form submissions come from real people, not bots.
  • Web3Forms — routes contact form submissions to my inbox.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

I do not sell your personal information. I only share it with the third-party service providers listed above, as necessary to operate the Site, or if required by law.

6. Data Retention

I retain contact form submissions and newsletter subscriber information for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or until you unsubscribe. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link in any email.
  • You can disable cookies through your browser settings — see the Cookie Policy.
  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing me (see Section 10).

8. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Data Security

I take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Me

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, email me at hi@nanayaaansah.com or use the contact form.
